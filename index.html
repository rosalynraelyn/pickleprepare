Privacy Policy for PicklePrepare

Last updated: July 7, 2026

PicklePrepare ("the App") is developed with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Data Collection

PicklePrepare does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or to any third party. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or an internet connection to function.

All information you enter into the App — including training session results, match history, self-assessment ratings, and notes — is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data, cannot see it, and cannot recover it if lost.

Third-Party Services

PicklePrepare does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

Because no data leaves your device, we do not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

PicklePrepare does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, as no data is collected at all.

Your Control Over Data

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by removing the App from your device, or by using any in-app delete/reset options provided.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version of the App adds optional cloud sync or account features — this page will be updated, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that change.
